About Tata Motors

Tata Motors Limited is a leading global automobile manufacturer and a member of the Tata Group, one of India’s most admired business conglomerates. Established in 1945, the company designs, manufactures, and markets a wide range of vehicles, including cars, trucks, buses, and defense vehicles. Tata Motors is known for its innovation and commitment to sustainability.

History

Tata Motors began its journey in 1945 as Tata Engineering and Locomotive Co. Ltd. (TELCO), focusing initially on the manufacturing of locomotives and other engineering products. In 1954, the company entered the commercial vehicle segment with the production of the first-ever truck, the Tata 16. After years of innovation and growth, Tata Motors became one of India’s leading automobile manufacturers.

Tata Motors’ journey to becoming an international giant included several significant milestones, such as its acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover in 2008, which brought iconic luxury car brands under its fold. Today, Tata Motors is an essential part of India’s automotive industry, with a global presence across various markets.

Vision

Tata Motors’ vision is to be the most admired and trusted automotive brand in the world, known for delivering superior value to customers, employees, and society. The company strives to be a global leader in innovation, sustainability, and the future of mobility.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of professionalism and ethics.
  • Innovation: Constantly evolving and improving through new technologies and solutions.
  • Customer-Centric: Always putting customers at the heart of the company’s operations.
  • Sustainability: Embracing practices that benefit the environment and society.
